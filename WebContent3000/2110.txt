The Alumni Council comprises a group of alumni focused on developing a strong social network for SPH students, recent graduates, and alumni. The Alumni Council focuses on increasing the visibility of UIC SPH and the network of alumni.  If you are interested in joining the Alumni Council please fill out the application below. Once submitted, a representative from the Alumni Council will reach out to discuss the opportunity further.
