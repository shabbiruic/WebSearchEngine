Some services provided are:    Moving of office furniture within a building  Relocating entire offices or departments  Emergency relocation of offices/departments due to moves or building renovations  Set up and provision of materials for special events  Delivery of freight too large or heavy to be handled by individual departments  Delivery of various publications to be distributed on campus  Pick-up and delivery of surplus furniture and equipment  Residence hall furniture inventory and redistribution  Lab equipment and perishable resource handling  Central Receiving Services   *Departments having a need for moving services must initiate a work order in the FAMIS system. Work requests should be made as far in advance as possible, because services will be provided on a first-come, first-service basis. However, every attempt will be made to accommodate specialized needs and emergencies.    Planning a MOVE only?   Prior to submitting an FMWeb Estimate Request, please contact the Facilities Management Movers at (312) 99 6-2841 or at moves@uic.edu to help you determine if an Estimate Request or one-time Billable Service Request is required.  Additionally, please refer to the UIC OBFS Standards for the Procurement, Transfer, Disposal, and Reallocation of University assets at https://www.obfs.uillinois.edu/bfpp/section-12-property-accounting/ .  To submit a request, please use the FMWeb system .
