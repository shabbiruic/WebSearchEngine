Industry relationships help sustain a collaborative learning environment that promotes engagement with company representatives and exposure to real-world challenges and conditions for students. Corporate philanthropic support also strengthens AHS in its efforts to broaden understanding of applied health sciences and disability and to expand opportunities for its students.  The AHS Corporate Partners Program provides numerous opportunities for its corporate members, including:  Access to AHS leadership to advise, provide industry perspective, and impact curriculum development.  Recruit a targeted student demographic through early access to expanded recruiting opportunities.  Collaborate with students and faculty on sponsored research projects and other opportunities.  Reach thousands of AHS students, faculty and staff via targeted marketing and branding strategies.  Receive access to AHS faculty and facilities for corporate consultations, meetings and focus groups.
