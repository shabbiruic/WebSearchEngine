About Health Policy and Administration   Health Policy and Administration (HPA) prepares future practitioners and researchers to improve the health and quality of care in our communities in Chicago, nationally and globally. HPA students learn from practitioner and research faculty; and gain qualitative and quantitative skills to analyze, manage, implement and improve public health. HPA prepares students for careers in changing the future of health, becoming public health policy analysts, healthcare administrators, clinical researchers, health economists and health service researchers.
