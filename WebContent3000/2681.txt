It is useful for Deans, Directors and Department Heads as well as their Student Employment Representatives who are responsible for the administration of student employees in their respective units. The terms "Student Employment" and "Job Center" used in this handbook refer to employment coordinated by the Student Employment Office.  Unless otherwise specified, information presented in this handbook applies to students working under both the Regular Student Employment (RSE) and the Federal Work Study (FWS) Programs. As changes occur, the information in these web pages will be changed accordingly.  If there are any questions, please contact Student Employment at: (312) 996-3130.
