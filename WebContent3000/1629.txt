Working with a Language Coach   What our language and writing coaches CAN do:   Help you organize a writing assignment  Give you feedback on the clarity of your thesis  Offer tips on genre-specific language use and word choices   What our language and writing coaches CANNOT do:   Correct or proofread your paper   What YOU should do to get the most out of your session:   Prepare specific questions.  Bring your book, workbook, or paper.  Bring a copy of the assignment.  In short, Come prepared .
