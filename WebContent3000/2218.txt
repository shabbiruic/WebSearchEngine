Degree requirements are listed below, and you will find the science education licensure requirements on the Council on Teacher Education website. Some of the courses required for licensure may be waived based on courses you may have previously completed.  Depending on the strand, a minimum of 32-36 semester hours of course credit are required for the degree, at least 9 of which must be at the 500 level.
