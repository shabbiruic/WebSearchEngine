Breadcrumbs   College of Education    Academics    Degree Programs    MEd Educational Policy Studies        The Educational Policy Studies concentration within the Instructional Leadership MEd program allows students to design their own programs of study or select courses related to three strands of study:   Educational Leadership and School Improvement  Education Policy and Policy Analysis  Social Justice and Education   The Policy Studies program offers students:   Opportunities for practicing educators to engage in professional development in the area of educational policy studies  An introduction to the field of education policy studies to persons who have recently completed their undergraduate degrees or who are contemplating a change of career  Opportunity to explore graduate education and a stepping stone to doctoral studies        Questions about the program?
