It invites conversations and discussions about a diversity of topics and transformations in the Arab region and among Arab American communities and other related diasporic spaces historically and in the present.   Halaqat Niqash (Discussion Sessions) : These discussion sessions will address a number of issues of immediate connection to the lives of students on campus and in relation to their identities. They will create a space for conversation, networking, and learning skills such as moderating discussion, developing ideas, public speaking, and cordial debating.  Nadwat (Public Presentations) : These will be educational events with invited guest speaker or speakers sharing specific insights on topics for collective engagement and exchange.
