The Academic Support and Achievement Program (ASAP) supports AHS undergraduate students through offerings of academic, social and career support. Our mission is to engage you in your academic curriculum, campus community and fields of study as a whole.  We encourage you to visit us as we hope you will engage in your environment and maximize your time at UIC. We want to help you achieve both academic and personal success in pursuit of your professional aspirations.  ASAP Core Values: a sense of belonging, academic engagement, professional development and ownership of learning.
