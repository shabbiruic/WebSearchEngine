Program Codes:  20FS1238MA    The Department of Anthropology offers work leading to the Master of Arts in Environmental and Urban Geography. The program has two major areas of study:   environmental analysis and monitoring, environmental behavior, and environmental management; and  urban geography, including the environmental impact of urbanization, industrial and commercial development, transportation, residential area analysis, and urban and regional structures.
