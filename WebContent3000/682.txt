PREZISTA  CAVE  SHINGRIX  DEEP  TICE         PREZISTA      The first treatment for multi-drug resistant HIV , Prezista is the number one prescribed protease inhibitor for patients who begin a new combination HIV Therapy. The compound was developed by former UIC professor Arun Ghosh and researchers from the National Institutes of Health (NIH). Prezista is the brand name of Darunavir, a protease inhibitor preventing HIV-infected cells from producing new virus and preventing drug-resistant mutations.
