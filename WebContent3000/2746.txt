Winter Break Emergency Info   If you experience violence over the holiday break, you are not alone, and help is available!  Our Campus Advocacy Network is closed December 24 – January 1.  Outside this break, advocates are available 9am – 5pm, Monday – Friday. Please call (312) 413-8206 or email can-appointment@uic.edu.  Who can you contact if you are in crisis?   Chicago Rape Crisis Hotline (888) 293-2080  National Sexual Assault Hotline (has a live chat option) (800) 656-4673  Illinois Domestic Violence Hotline (877) 863-6338  National Domestic Violence Hotline (has a live chat option) (800) 799-7233  More resources for counseling, advocacy and support   If you need immediate assistance:   Call UIC Police at (312) 355-5555, or  Dial 911   If you need emergency housing on campus:   During the business hours listed above, call CAN at (312) 413-8206.  Outside these hours, call UIC Police at (312) 355-5555.   If you need food:   The UIC Wellness Center’s Pop-Up Pantry offers free food and is open 1-4pm on December 18 & 19 and January 8 & 9.  For other options, visit the Greater Chicago Food Depository .
