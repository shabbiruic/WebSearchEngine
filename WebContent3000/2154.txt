Curriculum and Program Outcomes     Curriculum    Accounting is often called the language of business, and the core courses help develop fluency. Financial and cost accounting, auditing, tax, ethics, accounting systems and research build the foundation for understanding accounting as one of the most essential areas of business.  As you begin to learn more about accounting, you may choose to go into one of many specialized areas: corporate accounting, government accounting, nonprofit accounting, public accounting-tax or public accounting-audit. At UIC Business you will find a wide range of courses that help you move forward on the path that best fits your goals.  For a full list of curriculum requirements, visit the UIC Catalog .  For a full list of offered course, visit the course catalog.
