Breadcrumbs   School of Literatures, Cultural Studies and Linguistics    Foreign Languages at UIC    Foreign Languages Taught        Foreign Languages Taught  The School of Literatures, Cultural Studies and Linguistics offers basic language sequences in fourteen languages . Any of these sequences - with the exception of Portuguese -Â can be used to fulfill the LAS Foreign Language Requirement .  * For Chinese and Spanish , we offer separate language sequences designed specifically for heritage speakers (CHIN 111-112 and SPAN 113-114).  In this case, a heritage speaker is defined as a person who was exposed to Chinese or Spanish at home, but who was educated primarily in English.
