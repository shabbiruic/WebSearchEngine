Midwest Roybal Center  Tobacconomics   News          Methods for Investigating Heterogeneity in Intervention Effects: Beyond Traditional Tests of Moderation      This presentation was a non-technical overview of selected methods for exploring complex (e.g., multivariate, non-linear, asymmetric) sources of heterogeneity in the effects of health interventions.  The session included brief illustrative applications, highlighted practical resources for implementation, and discussed strengths and limitations of the methods, all from the perspective of an applied (non-statistician) researcher.
