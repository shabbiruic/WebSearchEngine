The Program consists of three parts:   Annual Global Youth Ambassadors Leadership Summit  Program Satellite Workshops  Mentorship and Alumni Engagement   Since its inception in 2016, more than 360 girls have participated in the overall Global Youth Ambassadors Leadership Program, with 95 of them participating in the Chicago Summit.  The Global Youth Ambassadors Leadership Program aims to:   Empower emerging leaders  Teach strategies for leadership, advocacy and activism  Inspire young women to become agents of change  Build a network of globally minded leaders   2020 Summit    The Global Youth Ambassadors Leadership Summit provides a globally-immersive experience for leadership development, cross-cultural awareness and civic exchange while building relationships to last a lifetime. The girls experience Chicago with their peers, while engaging with Chicagoâs business and civic leaders and participating in workshops, discussions and presentations on advocacy, activism and leadership.  Participants are selected via a competitive application process. The application is available to girls, ages 14-16, who live in or attend school in the city of Chicago or one of its 29 sister cities and have an interest in international relations, leadership, advocacy, activism or gender studies.
