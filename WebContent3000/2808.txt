Midwest Roybal Center  Tobacconomics   News              Advantages to UIC Researchers Who Work at the Institute      UIC scientists who conduct their research at IHRP benefit from the Institute's many strengths:   Outstanding support at all stages of research from study development through execution and onto new research questions and study designs.  Scientific support and review.  An integrated administrative team with years of experience and excellence in grants management, HR, IT and communications specific to a researcher’s needs.  Grants management expertise that begins with the proposal to ensure work can begin without delay upon notice of award.  Colleagues, resources and professional development for their research staff members.  High level of satisfaction with service and a collaborative ethos.  Freedom to work across boundaries and build multidisciplinary teams.
