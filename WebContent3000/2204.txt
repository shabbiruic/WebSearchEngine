Bilingual and English as a Second Language (ESL) Endorsements   The Bilingual and English as a Second Language (ESL) endorsements are for undergraduate or graduate students who are pursuing, or who have earned, an Illinois Professional Educator License. The endorsements enable students to teach in a Bilingual and/or English as a Second Language setting.    About the Endorsements         Middle Grades Endorsement   The College of Education offers courses that lead to a Middle Grades (5-8) Endorsement for current undergraduate and graduate students in licensure programs.
