The EMCP program is comprised of five 100% online graduate-level courses that can be completed in as few as three semesters.  The EMCP program is a collaboration between UIC’s School of Public Health and College of Business Administration that offers interdisciplinary leadership training in:   Communications Management  Continuity Planning  Crisis Management  Emergency Operations Management  Hazard Vulnerability Analysis  Information Systems  Organizational Crisis Response  Risk Management  Strategic Planning         Quote from Apostolis Sambias, PHD    The EMCP courses fulfill some of the required training contact hours for the certified emergency management certifications,Â AEMÂ®/CEMÂ® through the International Association of Emergency Managers .
