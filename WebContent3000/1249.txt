Master of Science in Marketing provides foundational and advanced knowledge of marketing, along with skills needed for a range of marketing positions and settings. Both strategic and tactical marketing knowledge and skills are cultivated, especially those pertinent to todayâs rapidly changing marketing environment, where innovation, technology, customer requirements, and competition are intensifying in speed and complexity. The program aims to produce marketing professionals prepared to work in the full range of marketing careers, including brand marketing, digital marketing, marketing communications, and marketing research and analytics.
