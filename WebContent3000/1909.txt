Advocate for Our Community.  UIC John Marshall Law School's clinical opportunities offer JD candidates the opportunity to develop real legal experience at the JD level. All clinics are designed to develop the courtroom-ready skills that employers seek in career applicants.  Read the General Clinic FAQs      Community Legal Clinics           Community Enterprise & Solidarity Economy Clinic  Assist individuals from underserved communities starting or growing cooperatives and other community-based businesses.             Fair Housing Legal Support Center & Clinic  Represent persons who have been denied housing in violation of federal, state, and local fair housing laws.             International Human Rights Clinic  Advocate for human rights in international and domestic tribunals and other forums.             IP Patent Clinic  Support innovation by helping inventors and small businesses protect their inventions.             IP Trademark Clinic  Assist individuals and small businesses who could not otherwise afford to protect their name, logo, or other mark.             Pro Bono Litigation Clinic  Represent individuals in federal and state courts in criminal and civil matters.             Veterans Legal Clinic  Serve those who served by providing legal assistance to veterans who have been improperly denied benefits.
