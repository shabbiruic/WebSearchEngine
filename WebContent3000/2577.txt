The minor is open to students from any college or major with sophomore standing who are not on academic probation. Students may apply credits from courses taken prior to application to the minor. At least 6 credits of the minor, including one elective, must be at the 200-level or higher. Students who wish to minor in Urban Studies shall notify the department in writing on the form posted on the UPP website.  Students in the College of Urban Planning and Policyâs undergraduate degree program in Urban Studies are not eligible to earn the Minor in Urban Studies. Some students may be eligible to earn both minors offered by the department (the Minor in GIS and the Minor in Urban Studies). However, students cannot apply the same course(s) to meet the requirements for both minors. A GPA of 2.50/4.00 is required in courses taken towards the minor. Courses applied to the Minor in Urban Studies may not apply to the Minor in Sustainable Cities or Minor in Geographic Information Systems.
