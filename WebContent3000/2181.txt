The following Master of Education programs offer guaranteed admission for BA Urban Education - Elementary Education students who meet specific qualifications:   MEd Early Childhood Education  MEd Educational Studies  MEd Language, Literacies and Learning  MEd Measurement, Evaluation, Statistics & Assessment (MESA)  MEd Special Education        GPPA Requirements    Membership in the UIC Honors College is strongly recommended, but not required. The Honors College Program offers GPPA students intellectual challenges that will help, them prepare for the rigors of graduate studies through focused individualized work on independent projects.  Students must maintain a minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA (A=4.0) each semester.  Students must maintain a full-time course load each semester (minimum of 12 semester hours of graded credits each semester).  Students must attend ONE meeting per semester with GPPA program faculty.  Students must have in interest in working in an urban setting.        GPPA Contact      Natalie
