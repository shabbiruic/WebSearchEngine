Fitness Station         Fitness Station   The fitness station, located near the 3rd base foul line beyond the outfield of the West Field, is a new addition to the OFC. The station is available for use during the normal operating hours of the OFC. The station may be unavailable when Campus Recreation is conducting a scheduled group fitness class at the OFC that utilizes the equipment.
