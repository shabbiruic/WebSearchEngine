The main office is accessible by entering the eastern part of SES at the level of the exterior crosswalk between the east and west parts of the building. Once inside the double glass doorways turn right to access the Department Office in Room 2440.  The department can be reached by car via the Eisenhower (I-290) and Dan Ryan (I-90/94) Expressways.
