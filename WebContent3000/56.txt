From Project HEAL, which has increased the number of Chicagoans who know their HIV status, to research investigating the efficacy of an app to improve HIV medication adherence among gay black men, UIC researchers are making a real impact on reducing the unintended spread of HIV. The program has expanded to include 20 Chicago beaches.
