Minimum Requirements for Admission  Minimum Admission Requirements:   2.0 UIC term GPA*  2.0 cumulative GPA*  One semester of UIC completed coursework  Adequate progress towards completion of degree   *Some majors require a different GPA for admission into the major. To review program-specific requirements, consult the LAS Departments sections on the left-hand side of the UIC Catalog.  All transfer coursework (even that which was previously reviewed by another UIC College) will be reviewed by LAS in order to have credit applied toward an LAS degree.  Application Timeline  All applications are reviewed after final grades post for the currently in-progress semester. Students will be notified of their application decision via their UIC email address one to two weeks after grades post , either prior to the start of the next semester or during the first week of the next semester . Admission becomes effective for the semester admitted, and tuition will be updated to reflect LAS fees once accepted.  Application Deadline (Summer 2020 Admission): Friday, May 8       Apply Here for Summer 2020 Admission       Intercollege Transfer Information Sessions   For the remainder of the Spring 2020 semester, LAS will not be offering in-person Information Sessions. Information for LAS ICT is available as an online presentation .  If you have additional questions or wish to meet with an academic advisor, you should RSVP for an Online Advising Session below.  Online Advising Sessions are not required to apply to LAS, however, it is the only way to meet with an LAS Advisor to have individual academic record reviewed.
