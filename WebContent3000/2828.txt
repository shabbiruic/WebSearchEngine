Midwest Roybal Center  Tobacconomics   News              Project Websites      The IHRP website lists all grants currently active at the Institute and those that have completed their funding cycle.  The nature of some research projects — that engage communities, recruit participants, and disseminate research materials — require separate, public-oriented websites. Such projects with current funding are listed below with links to their websites.  Asthma Action  Asthma Action at Erie is a research project to improve asthma management among low-income children in Chicago.  Cancer Education and Career Development Program  This fellowship program prepares promising new scholars to conduct research in the critical field of cancer disparities.  CO-OP Chicago  This study examines different approaches to improving the oral health of Chicago children.  Fit & Strong!  Fit & Strong is an intervention proven to increase physical activity and improve mobility among older adults with osteoarthritis.  Tobacconomics  This program is devoted to improving access to economic research and information that can impact and inform tobacco policy.
